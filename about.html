About Nathan Clicker

Welcome boys and girls to Nathan Clicker! The game where you click a sexy legend in the face and then spend the clicks to get more. You can use those clicks to buy upgrades and even gamble them if you have the balls. But to get more clicks, you have to purchase items! You lose some of your clicks when you buy an item, but you'll gain more clicks per second. And yes, Einstein, clicks per second are just what they sound like. So get out there, click some ass, and damn I love the alt key (laughs mercilessly).
